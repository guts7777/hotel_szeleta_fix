Hotel Szeleta ***

Hotel Szeleta*** is in Miskolc-Alsóhámor, inside the Bükk Mountains National Park, just 1 km from Lillafüred and 10 km from Miskolc city centre.

Sights around Hotel Szeleta

The hotel is extremely well located with many attractions within easy reach: the Lillafüred Forest Railway, the Szeleta Cave, the István Dripstone Cave, Lake Hámor, the Szinva waterfall, the preserved old ironworks, the Trout Farm in Garadna, and the City Zoo, all of which can be reached on foot. Short bus rides or car trips will get you to the famous Thermal Cave Baths in Tapolca, the Diósgyőr Castle, the Bánkút Ski Centre and other well-known sights.

The Hotel

The Hotel*** has 22 twin-bedded rooms and 3 suites. Guarded and partly-covered parking is provided for both cars and coaches. To make your stay with us that little bit more pleasant, all rooms have colour TV, mini-bar, telephone, en suite bathroom with shower and toilet, and Wi-Fi+PC Internet access. Included in the price of the room is a filling smorgasbord-style buffet breakfast.

A large, separate, air-conditioned room is available for family functions, conferences, lectures and presentations. The room will take 35-40 people, and is fully equipped with projector, flipchart and home cinema system, etc.

The price includes the breakfast, sauna, salt cave, outdoor pool, WiFi internet access services, parking, VAT and “DISCOUNT”.

Additional cost the Tourism Tax (“IFA” in hungarian) which is 450,-HUF/person/night (only above 18 years of age).

Check in time: from 2pm. Check out time: till 10am. The breakfast time is from 07:00 till 9:30. o’clock.

Because it is located at the eastern gateway to the Bükk Mountains, nestling in a beautiful valley surrounded by forests and on the banks of the river Szinva, Hotel Szeleta*** is perfect for all lovers of nature, tourism, winter sports and fishing. It’s also excellent for business people visiting Miskolc – after a tiring day what could be better for refreshing yourself and recharging your batteries than a few hours spent in beautiful countryside!
